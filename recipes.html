Mama June's Salisbury Steak

    Ingredients
  • Ground Sirloin
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Milk
      • Directions
  • Mix ground sirloin, panko bread crumbs, egg, milk, and 1/2 packet onion soup mix, Worcestershire sauce, and black pepper together in a large bowl; shape into 5 patties.
